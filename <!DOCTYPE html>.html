02. Typography

We use Montserrat. It is geometric, professional, and highly legible. Headings are typically bold/extrabold, while body text is light/regular.

Heading 1

text-6xl font-extrabold text-[#011A6B] tracking-tight

Heading 2

text-4xl font-bold text-[#011A6B]

Heading 3

text-2xl font-bold text-[#011A6B]

This is standard body paragraph text. It uses Slate-600 for softer contrast against the white background. Line height is relaxed to improve readability on complex B2B topics.

text-lg text-slate-600 leading-relaxed

03. Buttons & Interactions

Buttons must be rounded-full for primary actions and rounded-xl for secondary actions. We strictly avoid square buttons.

Primary Action

brand-gradient text-white rounded-full shadow-lg hover:shadow-xl hover:-translate-y-1 transition

Secondary Action

bg-white border-2 border-slate-200 rounded-xl hover:border-[#E8033A] transition

04. Cards & Containers

We use three types of card surfaces depending on the density of information.

Standard Card

Used for features, benefits, or grid layouts. White background, subtle border.

Glass Card

Used when placed over complex backgrounds or gradients. High transparency.

Gradient Border

Used for "Recommended" pricing tiers or active states. Requires the pseudo-element CSS method.

/* Standard Card Classes */
bg-white rounded-3xl border border-slate-100 shadow-sm hover:shadow-xl transition
